Intro

Hey!

My name's Logan and I'm a U3 Computer Science - Artificial Intelligence student at McGill University in Montreal, QC. I'm extremely interested in the theoretical and practical aspects of both AI and Software Development, and I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer and researcher. Feel free to look at my class projects (under "Education") and side projects (under "Projects") for a more specific idea of what I've been working on recently, or "About" to learn more about me and my personal life. If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to send me an email or connect with me on LinkedIn (both under "Links")!

Projects

  • On Sept 19, 2023 I noticed that I had an absurd amount of tabs open, many of which were duplicates- an issue that myself and many of my classmates experience almost daily. I decided to build a Chrome extension to solve this problem. A few hours later I submitted Tabulator to the Chrome Web Store, and it is now published and available for download! The source code is publicly available on my GitHub profile.
  • I'm currently working on a few projects:
    • A reinforcement learning agent, trained using Deep Q-Learning to maximize the agent's earnings in a game of Blackjack with varying shoe sizes, deck penetration, and numbers of players.
    • A data visualization project, using a custom data set I collected and cleaned from the book Red Rising, showcasing the social network of the characters in the book and their relationships with each other.
    • A desktop application in Python allowing users to track the amount of time they spend studying each subject per day, allowing them to allocate their time more effectively and efficiently.

    For a complete list of personal projects, please view my GitHub profile.
